What is FRC?

The Forest Rights Committee (FRC) is a key group formed within each Gram Sabha (village assembly) under the Forest Rights Act (FRA). The FRC is responsible for helping forest-dwelling and tribal communities assert their rights over forest land and resources by guiding them through the claims process.

What is FRA?

The Forest Rights Act (FRA), officially known as the Scheduled Tribes and Other Traditional Forest Dwellers (Recognition of Forest Rights) Act, 2006, is an important law in India aimed at recognizing the rights of tribal and other traditional forest-dwelling communities over the land and resources they have historically depended upon.
